Quality assurance process

The 4-Point Inspection System

An internationally recognized standard, executed with uncompromising accuracy across our global network of facilities.

straighten

1. Dimensional Stability

Precise measurement of rolls to ensure length, width, and weight conform to approved specifications.

palette

2. Shade Evaluation

Spectrophotometer analysis guarantees color consistency across dye lots and original standards.

search

3. Defect Mapping

Systematic visual scanning identifies weaving, finishing, or handling flaws before production advances.

score

4. Final Scoring

Penalty points determine lot acceptance before cutting, packing, and shipment release.
