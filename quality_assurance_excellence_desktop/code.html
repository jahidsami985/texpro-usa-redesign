Quality control process

Systematic QC from fabric inspection to final packing

Texpro Eco Apparel Ltd. follows a systematic quality control process from fabric inspection to final packing. Our experienced QC team monitors every production stage to ensure consistent workmanship, correct measurements, proper construction and buyer-required quality standards.

fact_check

In-process inspection and final audit based on AQL 2.5 or buyer-required standards

Inspection plans are aligned to buyer acceptance criteria and shipment expectations.

groups

Independent merchandising and quality control team

Production communication and QC review are handled by experienced teams.

texture

Fabric inspection to check shade, defects and consistency

Raw material checks help protect bulk consistency before cutting.

visibility

Inline quality monitoring during sewing production

QC teams monitor production stages to identify issues early.

straighten

Measurement and workmanship checking at each stage

Measurements, seams, trims, and construction details are checked throughout production.

inventory_2

Final inspection before packing and shipment

Final audit, packing, labeling, and carton readiness are reviewed before shipment.
