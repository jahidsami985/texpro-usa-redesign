Company overview

Outerwear, workwear, safety wear, and uniforms from Bangladesh

Texpro is a Bangladesh-based apparel manufacturer specializing in Outerwear, Workwear, High-Visibility Safety Wear and Uniforms. We support USA and European buyers with OEM and private-label manufacturing, full-package production, strong compliance, reliable quality control and timely delivery.

Our experienced merchandising, technical and quality control teams work closely with buyers from development to final shipment. We focus on product quality, clear communication, on-time delivery and long-term business partnerships.
