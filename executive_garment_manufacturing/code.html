Manufacturing process preview

From inquiry to controlled bulk production

A clear operating sequence helps buyers evaluate feasibility, approve details, and move into production with fewer surprises.

  1. 01 Inquiry

    Category and scope review

    Confirm category, target volume, fabric direction, customization needs, and delivery goals.

  2. 02 Development

    Sampling and approvals

    Align tech pack, materials, fit, trims, wash, packaging, and production specifications.

  3. 03 Production

    Line planning and execution

    Manage factory loading, inline inspection, finishing, packing, and status visibility.

  4. 04 Export

    Final QC and shipment

    Complete final checks, documents, carton preparation, and logistics handoff.
