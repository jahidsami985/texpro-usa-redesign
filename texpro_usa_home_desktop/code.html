Manufacturing Process

From product brief to export shipment

A controlled operating model keeps development, production, quality, and logistics aligned from the first sample to final garment packing.

  1. 01 Planning

    Program intake

    Review tech packs, target costs, materials, timelines, and compliance requirements before production begins.

  2. 02 Sourcing

    Materials and sampling

    Coordinate fabric, trims, fit samples, approvals, and production-ready specifications.

  3. 03 Production

    Bulk manufacturing

    Manage line loading, inline checks, capacity planning, finishing, packing, and buyer visibility.

  4. 04 Delivery

    Inspection and export

    Complete final quality checks, compliance documentation, and global logistics handoff.
